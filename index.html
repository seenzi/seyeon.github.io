Scuba Diving

My favorite hobby is scuba diving. I started it when I was in middle school because my parents recommended me to explore the deep sea world. My scuba buddy is my younger brother, taking care of each other and diving safely. My favorite sea creature is the sea snail. It is good to be able to focus entirely on the sea without outside noise when you go into the sea. It is a valuable experience to be able to explore the sea from a wide earth.

Musical & Movie

I like to watch music movies. Instead of expressing emotions with lines, I like music movies because of the part where the main characters express emotions with songs. When you listen to the OST album of the movie, you can feel the emotions of the main characters directly as the story of the movie is drawn in your head, and you can enjoy the emotions at the time when you watched the movie. My favorite music movie is La La Land. It is a movie that shows the color of the movie and the expression of emotions through the main characters' music. In addition, there are Sing Street, Mamma Mia, and Begin Again.
